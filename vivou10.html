Description

vivo U10. Powered by the Snapdragon 665 SoC, it boots Android Pie-based Funtouch OS 9.1 and comes in three memory versions - 3GB/32GB, 3GB/64GB, and 4GB/64GB. The phone also has a slot for microSD card, allowing storage expansion by up to 256GB. The U10 is built around a 6.35" 19.3:9 HD+ FullView Display, that has a notch up top housing the 8MP selfie camera. Around the back, it has a fingerprint reader keeping company to the triple camera setup aligned vertically in the top-left corner. This camera setup is a combination of a 13MP main, 8MP ultrawide angle, and 2MP depth sensor units.

The vivo U10 is offered in Thunder Black and Electric Blue colors and packs a 5,000 mAh battery with 18W fast charging support. It also comes with an Ultra Game Mode which includes features like 4D Vibrations and Voice Changer.

The U10 is priced at INR8,990 ($125/€115) for the 3GB/32GB variant and asks for INR1,000 ($15/€13) more to double the storage. The top-end model, with 4GB RAM and 64GB storage, will set you back INR10,990 ($155/€140). The U10 will go on sale in India from September 29 through the company's official site and Amazon.in.
