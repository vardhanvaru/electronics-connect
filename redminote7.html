Description

The Redmi Note 7S features a 6.3-inch FHD+ display with a water drop notch for the 13MP selfie cam. The device comes with Corning Gorilla Glass 5 on the front and back while the sides are made from aluminum. Powering the phone is the Qualcomm 660 chipset with a CPU running at 2.2 GHz paired with either 3GB or 4GB RAM and 32/64GB storage, expandable via microSD.

The highlight of the phone is around the back with its main 48MP f/1.8 camera. It uses the familiar Quad Bayer layer merging 4 pixels into 1 for effective 12MP photos. The primary shooter is helped by a 5MP f/2.4 depth sensor and features Night Mode with multiple HDR exposures for better low light photography. Battery wise we’re looking at a familiar 4,000 mAh cell with Quick Charge 4.0 support for 18W speeds. The box only includes a 10W charger though so if you need the higher speeds you’ll have to buy an extra adapter. The Redmi Note 7S runs Android 9.0 with MIUI 10 on top. Other extras include an IR blaster, 3.5mm headphone jack and a P2i water repellent nano-coating.

The Redmi Note 7S will come in Onyx Black, Sapphire Blue and Ruby Red. Pricing starts at INR 9,999 ($158) for the 3/32GB model while the 4/64GB variant goes for INR 10,999 ($187). The device will be available starting May 23 online via Xiaomi’s website and Flipkart.

The Redmi Note 7S is a re-branded version of the Chinese Note 7. It's powered by Snapdragon 660 SoC and sports a 6.3-inch FHD+ LCD. It runs Android Pie-based MIUI 10 out-of-the-box and boasts a dual camera setup at the back which is a combination of 48MP and 5MP units. The Redmi Note 7S packs a 4,000 mAh battery and comes in three colors - Ruby Red, Onyx Black, and Sapphire Blue.
